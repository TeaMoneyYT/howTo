Eggs

Ingredients

  • Stove pan
  • Spatula
  • Cooking oil/cooking spray(preferably non-stick)
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Put your pan on your stove and light on medium heat.
  2. Use your cooking spray on the pan, thoroughly spraying the pan so the eggs don't stick to the inside.
  3. Take your egg/eggs and crack over the pan, so that the insides fall into the pan.
  4. Wait a few minutes, checking the eggs to see if they need flipped. Once ready, flip the eggs.
  5. Once both sides are cooked, turn the stove off and put the eggs onto your plate, and enjoy!
